Everything your finance team needs

From daily invoicing to CFO-level analytics — Leadger covers the full accounting lifecycle without the complexity.

🧾

Sales & Purchases

Create invoices with IVA and retención, manage purchase orders, and auto-generate journal entries. Supports PGC España and US GAAP simultaneously.

🏦

Treasury & Reconciliation

Import bank statements, reconcile movements against invoices or remittances, and manage wire transfers. Real-time cash position from Journal Entries.

📒

Double-Entry Bookkeeping

Every transaction generates a balanced journal entry automatically. Full audit trail, drill-down from P&L to individual accounts.

Tax Management

Track IVA repercutido, IVA soportado, and retenciones by quarter. Quarterly summary ready for Modelo 303, 111, and 115 preparation.

HR & Payroll

Manage employees, generate payrolls with SS, IRPF and net calculations, and post directly to journal entries. Org chart with reporting hierarchy.

CFO Intelligence
